What you can do

Chat-Based Search

Natural language to Apollo filters. Describe your ideal customer in plain English and the AI translates it into precise search parameters — no forms, no dropdowns.

AI Fit Scoring

GPT-4o scores every lead 0-100 based on how well they match your ideal customer profile. Each score includes an explanation so you know exactly why a lead ranks high or low.

Auto-Pilot

Daily prospecting runs on schedule. Set your ICP once and the system finds new leads every day, scores them, and adds them to your pipeline — no manual effort required.

Quality Filters

Exclude garbage, require company data, filter by employee count, revenue, and industry. Only qualified leads make it through to your pipeline.

Say "Midwest" and the AI expands it to IL, WI, MN, IN, OH, MI. Regional targeting made simple — no need to list every state manually.
