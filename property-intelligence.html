Property Intelligence

Know the property before the first call.

AI analyzes satellite imagery, street view, and Google Maps interior photos to assess every property. Industry-specific metrics — solar gets kW and ROI, landscaping gets turf and zones.

3-stage intelligence: Geo → Property → Service Mapping

Satellite imagery with property analysis

What you can do

Satellite Tiles

Multi-zone coverage for large properties. AI stitches multiple satellite tiles together to analyze entire commercial complexes, not just a single viewport.

Street View

Building exterior assessment from Google Street View. AI evaluates building condition, signage, parking, and accessibility without leaving your desk.

Interior Photos

Google Maps business photos analyzed by AI. Understand interior layout, equipment, fixtures, and condition — all from publicly available imagery.

Viewport Sizing

Measured acreage from Google geocoding. Get accurate property size estimates so you can quote confidently before the site visit.

Industry Metrics

Solar: kW capacity, kWh generation, savings estimates. HVAC: tonnage requirements. Landscaping: turf area, zones. Metrics tailored to your industry.

Opportunity Score

A-D grade with dollar estimates. AI evaluates property condition, size, and service fit to rank opportunities by revenue potential and close probability.
