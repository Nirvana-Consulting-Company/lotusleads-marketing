What you can do

Enriched Contacts

Apollo data: email, phone, LinkedIn profile, job title, and company details. Every lead comes with the contact information you need to reach decision-makers.

Inline Editing

Update any field directly on the lead card. Change status, add notes, correct contact info, or update company details without opening a separate form.

Pipeline Status

New → Contacted → Qualified → Won. Track every lead through your sales process with clear stage progression and conversion visibility.

Ready Inbox

Fully analyzed leads waiting for action. The Ready tab filters out leads still being enriched and shows only those with complete data, scores, and property analysis.

Activity Timeline

Every email, call, note, and status change tracked in chronological order. See the complete history of every interaction with a lead at a glance.

CSV Export

Download leads for external tools. Export filtered lead lists with all enriched data to CSV for use in other CRMs, spreadsheets, or reporting tools.
