What you can do

AI Email Generation

Every email unique, contextual, and ready to send. GPT-4o crafts personalized messages using property data, industry fit, and company research — no templates.

Multi-Step Sequences

3-step campaigns with configurable delay scheduling. First touch, follow-up, and final outreach — each step tailored to the lead's engagement level.

Auto Follow-ups

Cron sends step 2 and step 3 automatically on schedule. No manual intervention needed — the system handles timing and delivery through Resend.

Pause / Resume

Control campaigns without losing progress. Pause a running campaign instantly, make adjustments, and resume exactly where you left off.

Per-Lead Tracking

Sent, scheduled, and replied status for every lead in the campaign. See exactly which step each prospect is on and who has engaged.

Activity Logging

Every email appears in the lead's activity timeline. Full audit trail of campaign interactions alongside manual outreach and notes.
