Frequently Asked Questions

Is this really free?
Yes, 100% free for property managers. There's no obligation to hire any of the contractors who provide quotes. Service providers pay for the platform — you never will.
How are contractors vetted?
We verify licensing, insurance, and commercial experience for every provider on our platform. We also monitor reviews and service quality through our network.
How quickly will I hear back?
Most property managers receive their first quote within a few hours. You'll typically have 2–4 competitive proposals within 24 hours of submitting your request.
What types of properties can I request quotes for?
Any commercial property — office buildings, retail centers, industrial facilities, medical complexes, hotels, multi-family residential, government buildings, campuses, and more. We focus exclusively on commercial and institutional properties.
