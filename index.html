How It Works

Four steps to more revenue

From defining your ideal customer to closing deals — LotusLeads automates the entire pipeline.

1

Describe Your Ideal Customer

Tell AI who you want to find. Describe your ideal customer in plain English — industry, location, company size, property type — and our AI builds a precision targeting profile instantly.

  • Natural language ICP definition — no forms or filters
  • AI interprets intent and builds search parameters
  • Target any geography, industry, or property type
  • Save and reuse profiles for ongoing prospecting
2

AI Scores & Ranks Results

See who's worth your time. Every lead is automatically enriched with company data, contact information, and scored by fit — so your team focuses on the highest-value opportunities first.

  • Intelligent lead scoring based on ICP match
  • Auto-enrichment with emails, phones, and decision-makers
  • Ranked results so your best leads surface first
  • Duplicate detection and CRM deduplication
3

Deep Property Intelligence

Know the property before the first call. AI analyzes satellite imagery, building footprints, roof conditions, lot size, and more — giving your reps property-specific talking points before they ever pick up the phone.

  • Satellite imagery and aerial property analysis
  • Building footprint, roof area, and lot size data
  • Property-specific talking points for every lead
  • Condition scoring for roof, landscape, and HVAC opportunities
4

Automated Outreach

Personalized campaigns that run themselves. AI drafts hyper-personalized email sequences using property data, company intel, and industry context — then sends them on your schedule.

  • AI-written emails personalized with property data
  • Multi-step sequences with automated follow-ups
  • Smart scheduling for optimal open rates
  • Reply detection and automatic sequence pausing
