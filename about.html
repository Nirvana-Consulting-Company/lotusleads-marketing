Our Story

Finding the right contractor
shouldn't be this hard.

LotusLeads is the marketplace that connects property owners with trusted, verified service professionals — powered by AI that makes matching smarter, estimates faster, and decisions easier.

Before LotusLeads, our founders spent years helping service companies grow — and saw the same problem from both sides. Property owners struggled to find trustworthy contractors. And great contractors struggled to get found by the right customers.

The process was broken everywhere: property owners were stuck cold-calling from Google results, reading fake reviews, and getting wildly inconsistent quotes. Meanwhile, skilled service companies — roofers, landscapers, HVAC techs, solar installers — were invisible to the property managers and business owners who needed them most.

"We kept hearing the same thing from both sides: property owners saying 'I can't find anyone good,' and contractors saying 'I can't get in front of the right customers.' The marketplace to connect them simply didn't exist — so we built it."

— Rahul Surana, Founder

What if you could search your property address, see an instant AI-powered cost estimate, and connect with the best-rated local contractors — all in under 60 seconds? And what if those contractors had a verified profile, real reviews, and the tools to respond fast?

LotusLeads is that marketplace. We've built a directory of over 24,000 verified contractors across 12 industries, paired with AI-powered property estimation, instant quote requests, and intelligent matching that considers ratings, proximity, and specialization.

For service companies, we provide the growth platform they've always needed: a verified profile that builds trust, qualified leads from property owners actively looking for services, and business tools — quoting, invoicing, scheduling, and campaign management — all in one place.

Rahul Surana

Founder & CEO

Rahul spent over a decade in business transformation consulting, helping companies modernize operations and build digital-first strategies. He saw the same problem across every field service company he worked with: great contractors with no way to get discovered by the right customers.

That insight led to LotusLeads — a marketplace that levels the playing field. Instead of relying on word-of-mouth or expensive advertising, any service company can build a verified profile, receive qualified leads, and grow their business through the platform.

He founded Nirvana Consulting Company to bridge the gap between enterprise capability and small business accessibility — and LotusLeads is the flagship product bringing that vision to life across 12 service industries.

Nick Merican

Co-Founder & Product Owner

Nick brings deep product thinking and a relentless focus on what actually makes the marketplace work for both sides. As Product Owner, he shapes every feature through the lens of the end user — whether that's a property manager requesting quotes or a contractor managing their pipeline.

His background in business strategy gives him a dual perspective: he understands the property owner who needs transparency and trust, and the service company that needs qualified leads without the runaround of traditional lead-gen platforms.

Nick's product philosophy is simple: if a property owner can't go from search to estimate to connected with a pro in under a minute, the marketplace isn't doing its job. That standard drives every feature, from the AI cost estimator to instant quote requests.

What Drives Us

Built on fairness, not middleman margins.

Small Business Empowering Small Business

We're a small business ourselves. We built LotusLeads because the biggest contractors had all the visibility while independent operators — often the best at their craft — were invisible online. The marketplace levels that playing field.

No Pay-to-Play Rankings

Other platforms sell top placement to the highest bidder. We rank contractors by ratings, reviews, and proximity — the factors that actually matter to property owners. A 5-person crew with great reviews outranks a national chain with mediocre ones.

Transparency Over Friction

Property owners see real reviews, real ratings, and AI-powered cost estimates before they ever talk to a contractor. Contractors get real leads from property owners who are actively looking — not recycled contact lists sold to five competitors.
