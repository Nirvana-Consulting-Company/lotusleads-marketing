Our Story

We didn't set out to build software.
We set out to fix a broken process.

LotusLeads was born from years of watching field service companies leave millions on the table — not because they lacked talent, but because they lacked the tools.

Before LotusLeads, our founders spent years deep in the trenches of business transformation — helping companies overhaul their operations, modernize their workflows, and compete in markets that were leaving them behind. From ERP migrations to digital sales enablement, they saw the same pattern over and over again.

The biggest companies had armies of analysts, expensive data providers, and custom-built CRM integrations. They could identify a prospect, analyze the property, research the competition, and send a personalized proposal — all before a smaller competitor even finished their first Google search.

"The field service companies we worked with weren't losing deals because their work was bad. They were losing because they couldn't find the right prospects fast enough, didn't understand the property before the first call, and had no competitive intelligence to sharpen their pitch."

— Rahul Surana, Founder

That gap — between the intelligence the enterprise players had and what everyone else was working with — became the mission. What if a 10-person landscaping crew could walk into a sales meeting with the same property data, competitive analysis, and personalized pitch that a national chain had?

LotusLeads is the answer. We took everything we learned from transforming businesses across industries — the workflows that actually work, the data that actually matters, the automations that actually save time — and built it into a single platform purpose-built for B2B field service sales.

We didn't build another CRM. We built the intelligence layer that sits on top of your sales process: AI-powered prospecting, satellite property analysis, competitive research, service mapping, and automated outreach — all working together so your team spends less time researching and more time closing.

RS

Rahul Surana

Founder & CEO

Rahul spent over a decade leading business transformation engagements — helping companies rethink their operations from the ground up. From enterprise ERP implementations to digital-first go-to-market strategies, he's seen what separates companies that scale from those that stall.

That experience revealed a consistent gap in the field services market: sales teams with deep industry expertise but no data infrastructure to match. The best reps were still doing manual research, cold-calling from spreadsheets, and walking into meetings without property intelligence or competitive context.

He founded Nirvana Consulting Company to solve exactly these kinds of problems — and LotusLeads became the flagship product: a platform that brings enterprise-grade sales intelligence to the teams that need it most.
