What's included in the 14-day free trial?
Full access to all features on your selected plan — no credit card required. You can cancel at any time before the trial ends and you won't be charged.
What AI features are included on every plan?
All plans include AI Property Analysis and Service Mapping. Professional and Enterprise add AI Message Generation, Opportunity Scoring, and Perplexity Research.
Can I upgrade or downgrade anytime?
Yes. Change plans at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at the end of your billing cycle. No penalties, no lock-ins.
How does the annual discount work?
Choosing annual billing saves you 20% compared to monthly. You're billed once per year upfront. The discount applies immediately when you switch.
What counts as a 'lead'?
A lead is a unique contact discovered or imported in a given billing period. Your monthly limit resets on each billing cycle. Unused leads don't roll over.

Yes — if you're unhappy within the first 30 days of a paid subscription, contact us and we'll issue a full refund, no questions asked.
